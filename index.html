Welcome to Sare Ngang'o

The Sare Ngang’o Initiative is a grassroots movement founded in 2025 to combat chang’aa abuse, empower youth, and restore dignity in Kenyan communities through awareness, skills training, and faith-based support.

About Us

Vision: To build a drug-free, empowered, and self-reliant community through education, counseling, and economic empowerment.

Mission: To rescue individuals from the harmful effects of chang’aa and other destructive habits by offering rehabilitation, mentorship, and support through faith-based and grassroots interventions.

Core Areas:

  • Anti-Chang’aa Awareness Campaigns
  • Counseling and Rehabilitation
  • Youth Skills Training and Mentorship
  • Alternative Income Projects
  • Faith-Based Community Engagement

Programs

  • 21-Day Anti-Chang’aa Campaigns
  • Community Counseling Forums
  • Graphic Design & Digital Skills Bootcamps
  • Vocational Training: Farming, Crafts, Small Business
  • Family Healing & Church Engagement Events

Strategic Plan (2025–2027)

2025: Registration, awareness campaigns, community mobilization
2026: Launch skills programs, support groups, donor partnerships
2027: Establish permanent recovery & training center
